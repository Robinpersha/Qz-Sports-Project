About us

The Q'z Sports Hub isn't a business; it's a collaborative, community-driven initiative committed to enhancing community sport. This online platform represents just one of many initiatives stemming from the collective voices of our community members. Supported by provincial and municipal partners and powered by dedicated volunteers, it provides numerous opportunities for active engagement within our communities.
